Worksheet A: Message Marker Identification

  1. ________. What are your intentions?
  2. ________. I will alter course to starboard.
  3. ________. You are running into danger.
  4. ________. I require pilot assistance.
  5. ________. My present course is one-eight-zero degrees.

Worksheet B: Phrase Correction

Rewrite the phrases in SMCP style.

  1. Turn right.
  2. Can you repeat that?
  3. We need a doctor fast.
  4. The ropes are okay.
  5. There is something floating over there.
