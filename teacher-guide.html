5. Common Remediation Notes

  • If students overuse Information, ask them: “Is this a fact, order, danger, request, or plan?”
  • If students use left/right, require immediate correction to port/starboard.
  • If students say OK, require a full read-back of the action and object.
  • If students struggle with emergency messages, give them a fixed order: identity → position → problem → assistance → persons on board.
  • If students misread numbers, return to the Listening & Pronunciation Lab.
