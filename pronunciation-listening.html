Listening and Pronunciation Lab

This lab gives students focused practice with the sounds and number-reading patterns that cause communication problems on VHF.

3. Listening Scripts for Classroom Use

Script A - Routine Traffic: Information. My present position is bearing zero-eight-five degrees, distance two decimal four miles from Buoy Charlie. Question. What is the traffic situation?

Script B - Emergency: PAN-PAN, PAN-PAN, PAN-PAN. Information. I have steering failure. Request. I require tug assistance.

Script C - Collision Avoidance: Warning. Risk of collision exists. Question. What are your intentions? Intention. I will reduce speed and pass astern of you.

Script D - Warning: SECURITE. Warning. Unlit buoy reported in position one-three degrees zero-five minutes North, one-zero-zero degrees four-eight minutes East. Advice. Keep clear.
